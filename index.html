Our Goal

We are passionate UCLA students researching how certain marine restoration projects, including kelp forests and seagrass beds, can provide various benefits for both the planet and its inhabitants. As part of UCLA'S Institute of the Environment and Sustainability, our practicum goal is to establish a scientific communications portal for students and researchers to utilize for future restoration.
